Chess Timer Professional

Privacy policy for the iOS mobile application.

Last updated: November 2025 Developer: Necodeveloper Platform: iOS

Chess Timer Professional (“the App”) is developed by Necodeveloper. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. No Data Collected

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection tools inside the App.

2. No Third-Party Access

The App does not access your contacts, photos, files, location, identifiers, or any other personal information on your device.

3. App Functionality

Chess Timer Professional runs entirely on your device and functions only as a chess clock. All timing and game information remains on the device and is not sent to any server controlled by us.

4. Advertising

The App may display banner or interstitial ads using Google AdMob. While the App itself does not collect data, Google may process information as part of its standard ad delivery and measurement services.

For details on how Google handles data, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be communicated through an updated app release.
