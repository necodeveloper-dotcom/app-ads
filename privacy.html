Privacy Policy for Chess Timer Professional

Last updated: November 2025

Chess Timer Professional (“the App”) is developed by Necodeveloper. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. No Data Collected

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection tools.

2. No Third-Party Access

The App does not access your device information, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal data.

3. App Functionality

The App functions entirely offline and only serves as a chess timer. All operations happen on your device and no information leaves the device.

4. Ads

The App may display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data as part of its standard ad delivery process. For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for all ages and does not collect any personal data from children.
